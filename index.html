Wae Rebo

NTT

Wae Rebo is a traditional village located high in the Manggarai Mountains, Flores. Dubbed the "Village Above the Clouds," Wae Rebo offers an atmosphere unmatched in the city: serenity, coolness, and the warmth of local culture. Its distinctive cone-shaped traditional house, the Mbaru Niang, is an icon that makes this village truly unique.
